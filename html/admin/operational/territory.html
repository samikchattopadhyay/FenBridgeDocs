Screen 31Territory Management Dashboard
territory_management_dashboard
Purpose
Enable BM Operations staff and Regional Managers to monitor territory health — farmer distribution by village, KYC SLA compliance, bid fill rate, and scout workload. Identify underperforming areas and predict bottlenecks before they impact fulfillment.
Business Requirements
• BR-123: Real-time farmer density per village (target: 50+ farmers/village).
• BR-124: KYC approval SLA monitoring (target: <48h).
• BR-125: Bid fill rate by region (target: >85%).
• BR-126: Identify low-activity villages needing outreach.
• BR-127: Scout workload balancing (target: 50 farmers/scout/month, max 10 ponds/day).
Entry Point
BM Dashboard → "Territory Health" tab · Or: Admin Portal → "Regional Operations" → Select region.
UX & User Actions
• Select region/territory from dropdown or click map.
• Heat map: farmer density by village (green ≥50, yellow 20–50, red <20).
• Scout pins: blue (good capacity), orange (near capacity), red (overloaded).
• Click village marker → detail popup (farmers, KYC pending, fill rate).
• Actions: Assign/reassign scouts, send outreach, escalate SLA breaches.
Database Schema
territory_health_summary ( territory_id TEXT PK, region TEXT, village TEXT, farmer_count INT, kyc_sla_pct DECIMAL(5,2), bid_fill_rate DECIMAL(5,2), scout_id UUID FK -> users.id, scout_utilization_pct DECIMAL(5,2), alert_flags JSONB, updated_at TIMESTAMP ); GET /api/v1/territory/dashboard?region=&period=
