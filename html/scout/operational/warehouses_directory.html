Key Design Patterns

Fitness Status Impact
Pass = Warehouse eligible to hold BM lots. Fail = Blocks new lot sealing at this warehouse until issues resolved.
Failure Analysis
Storage-failure history shows pattern of defects linked to this warehouse. Helps identify systematic issues.
Scope Limitation
Scout sees only warehouses assigned to them. No cross-territory visibility. Enforced server-side.
Inventory Tracking
Real-time lot status visible (sealed, ready, sold, voided). Helps scout manage warehouse capacity and monitor shelf-life.
