Workflow Module 4

Popper Registration

Scout-assisted 18-step wizard to register new popper (buying/processing facility) into system. Captures business identity, warehouse details, bank account, legal documents with all records entering pending approval status until admin review.

Registration Steps
18 Steps
Step 1Intro & Consent
Purpose
Scout explains registration process and obtains verbal consent from popper.
Requirements
  • Consent checkbox: "I understand popper will be pending approval"
  • Option to cancel and exit
Step 2Contact Person Identity
Capture
  • Contact person's full name
  • Date of birth (optional)
  • Mobile number (mandatory)
Step 3Mobile Verification — OTP Send
Purpose
Verify popper's mobile is valid and scout present for consent.
Process
  • Scout triggers OTP send to popper's mobile
  • Scout reads OTP aloud; popper confirms receipt
Step 4Mobile Verification — OTP Entry
Purpose
Scout enters OTP on popper's behalf (scout present).
Outcome
  • OTP verification success: mobile confirmed authentic
  • OTP failure: user prompted to retry (max 3 attempts)
Step 5Language Selection
Options
  • Language: English / Hindi / Bengali
Step 6Confirm Role = Popper
Purpose
Confirm popper type and primary business operation.
Role Options
  • Processor (processes/pops raw makhana)
  • Trader (buys finished makhana for resale)
  • Warehouse (stores makhana on behalf of others)
  • Multi-role (any combination)
Step 7Address Details
Capture
  • District, sub-district, village
  • Street address (free-text)
  • Postal code (if available)
Step 8Business Basic Details
Capture
  • Business legal name
  • GSTIN (if registered)
  • FSSAI license number (if applicable)
Step 9Business Legal Documents
Capture
  • GSTIN certificate (if registered) - 1-3 photos
  • FSSAI license (if applicable) - 1-3 photos
  • Business registration / factory license - 1-3 photos
Step 10Business Address Details
Capture
  • District, sub-district, village
  • Street address
  • Postal code
Step 11First Warehouse Address
Capture
  • District, sub-district, village
  • Street address
  • Postal code
Step 12First Warehouse Details
Requirement
Popper must have at least one warehouse to transact.
Capture
  • Warehouse name, capacity (kg/bags), type (processor/trader/storage)
Step 13First Warehouse Photos
Capture
  • Lease agreement OR ownership deed (1-3 photos)
  • Interior & exterior warehouse photos (up to 5 photos)
Step 14First Warehouse Geo-Fencing (GPS)
Purpose
Capture GPS coordinates for warehouse location.
Requirements
  • Real-time GPS from device (scout must be at warehouse)
  • Accuracy reading in meters (usually 5-15m)
  • GPS auto-stamped to photos
Step 15Bank Account Details
Capture
  • Account holder name (must match popper business name or authorized signatory)
  • Account number (entered twice for confirmation)
  • IFSC code
Process
  • Penny-drop verification triggered server-side after submit
Step 16Theme Preference
Options
  • Theme: Dark / Light
Step 17Review & Submit
Display
Summary of all entered data. Scout reviews with popper for accuracy.
Actions
  • Edit (go back to specific step) OR Submit
  • Confirmation checkbox before submit
Step 18Submission Confirmation
Status
Popper registration submitted. Status: pending_approval. All records queued to sync_queue.
Next Steps
  • Admin reviews registration within 24-48 hours
  • Penny-drop verification runs server-side (results arrive in minutes)
  • Admin approves/rejects based on documents and bank verification
  • Popper notified of approval status

Popper Registration Requirements & Approval Gates

Pending Approval Status
Popper registered but status locked as pending_approval. Cannot list/sell until admin approves. May take 24-48 hours.
Mandatory Warehouse
Popper must have at least one warehouse at registration. Warehouse also enters pending_approval. Fitness baseline captured at registration.
Penny-Drop Verification
Bank account verified via automatic penny-drop test (deposits 1-2 rupees). Results land in minutes. Required for payout eligibility after approval.
Offline-First & Immutability
All data captured locally; documents compressed ≤1.5MB. Once submitted, records immutable by scout. Admin-only corrections via backend.
