Workflow Module 6

Re-Inspection

Follow-up verification of reconditioned lots post-rejection. Scout confirms remediation success against prior defect. Success restores lot to previous state; failure immediately voids lot. Linked to rejection record for full audit trail.

Re-Inspection Steps
5 Steps
Step 1Rejection Context & Defect Review
Prior Rejection
Scout shown prior rejection reason, coded category, and evidence photos. Displays defect area and severity assessment from original rejection.
Timeline
Re-inspection scheduled within 7 days of rejection. Farmer had time to recondition (dry, re-grade, re-bag, etc.). Expired re-inspection tasks auto-void lot.
Step 2Recondition Verification
Verification Check
  • Scout visually inspects lot to confirm defect remediated
  • Compares current state against prior rejection photos
  • Assesses if remediation was effective (defect no longer present)
  • Documents any residual issues or new defects discovered
Coded Assessment
  • Defect Fully Remediated (original issue resolved)
  • Partially Remediated (some issue remains, marginal)
  • Not Remediated (defect unchanged or worse)
  • New Defects Found (different issue emerged)
Step 3Evidence Photos & Notes
Remediation Evidence
Minimum 2-3 mandatory photos showing reconditioned lot state. Match angle/distance of original rejection photos for comparison. Geo-stamped, ≤1.5MB each.
Inspector Notes
Optional detailed assessment (≤500 chars) explaining whether remediation successful and confidence level in restored quality.
Step 4Verdict Decision
Pass Verdict
  • Defect fully remediated; lot restored to previous state
  • Unsealed lot: status restored to active (can list/bid again)
  • Sealed lot: status restored to sealed (can transact)
  • PO lot: status restored to ready_for_verification or in_progress
  • Buyer re-notified that lot is available again
Fail Verdict
  • Defect not remediated or worsened; lot voided immediately
  • Farmer notified of failure; no further appeals
  • Lot removed from all marketplaces
  • No recovery pathway; financial loss to farmer
Step 5Re-Inspection Completion & Notification
Confirmation Screen
  • Re-inspection verdict confirmed (Pass/Fail)
  • Lot status updated in real-time
  • Scout receives confirmation of verdict recorded
Notifications
  • Farmer notified immediately of pass/fail verdict
  • Buyer alerted (if PO: lot available again OR lot cancelled)
  • Admin sees full re-inspection evidence in audit trail
  • Records queued to sync_queue for offline persistence

Re-Inspection Workflow & Linked Context

Rejection Link
Re-inspection task created during rejection disposition. Full prior defect context visible to scout. Photos compared side-by-side with original rejection evidence.
Time Window
7-day deadline from rejection. Expired tasks auto-void lot without appeal. Farmer notified of deadline at rejection time.
Status Restoration
Pass verdict restores lot to pre-rejection state (active, sealed, or in_progress). PO buyers automatically re-notified. Spot offers re-enabled.
Immutability & Audit
Re-inspection record immutable. Full chain: Rejection → Recondition → Re-Inspect verdict. All evidence preserved for compliance and dispute resolution.
