01. Executive Summary & Core Objective

The FenBridge platform (operating as a digital nexus for Bharat Makhana) is engineered to formalize and capture value within fragmented rural agricultural supply chains—specifically focusing on pond-based raw Popped Makhana procurement across target production nodes in West Bengal and Bihar.

By transitioning away from conventional unregulated commission models, FenBridge institutes an aggressive digital infrastructure combining high-fidelity physical testing frameworks with secure algorithmic financial settlements. The system ensures robust end-to-end pricing traceability, transparent localized supply assessments, and zero-liability fulfillment structures.

02. Principal Trader Business Model

To minimize complex regulatory overheads during initial scale cycles, FenBridge firmly operates under a Principal Trader entity model rather than an open marketplace framework. Title of physical goods transfers directly from primary producers to Bharat Makhana, and subsequently clears straight to terminal processors upon destination acceptance.

Zero Marketplace Liability

Eliminates the requirement for complex Payment Aggregator (PA) or Payment Gateway (PG) regulatory escrow licenses by processing all disbursements natively via dedicated commercial clearing accounts.

Spread-Based Revenue Anchoring

Monetizes procurement velocity directly through optimized regional spread margins rather than platform subscription fees or transaction fee commissions.

Conditional Title Assignment

Retains formal Reservation of Right of Disposal clauses operational until ultimate settlement milestones clear post-gate verification.

04. Regulatory & Tax Optimization Pillars

The corporate entity structure and transaction documents are fundamentally designed to anchor long-term fiscal efficiency and audit defensibility:

Section 44AD Presumptive Taxation

Incorporated formally as a registered Partnership Firm entity. Secures maximum compliance benefits by reporting predictable deemed margins on gross turnovers up to threshold ceilings, deferring complex balance-sheet overheads to later enterprise expansions.

GST Exemption Preservation

Operates strictly within raw, unbranded agricultural produce mandates mapped cleanly under Section 23(1)(a). Procurement logs dispatch using structured non-taxable Bills of Supply accompanied by Unregistered Person (URP) E-Way bill keys.

05. Inventory Seal & Dispute Safeguards

To eliminate subjective destination rejections, the platform enforces strict physical hardware-to-software inventory tracking loops alongside automated four-stage arbitration triggers:

Every approved lot receives a custom reusable physical net secured with sequential tamper-evident barcodes linked directly to source purchase orders. In case of destination gate quality disputes raised within the mandatory 48-hour resolution horizon, the system activates an automated protocol:

  • 1. Hardware Integrity Verification: Instantly cross-checks unboxing logs against source barcode seals.
  • 2. Primary Locker Sample Extraction: Compares delivery properties against immutable regional field samples.
  • 3. NABL Laboratory Routing: Mandates objective scientific analysis of moisture, expansion ratios, and sizing via certified facilities.
  • 4. Ledger/Escrow Freeze Automation: Suspends pending settlement dispatches to protect underlying capital during assessment.

To enforce security parameters, uphold absolute operational accountability, and support specialized field execution, the platform decouples business activities into five targeted functional domains covering exactly 20 end-to-end workflows:

Common Foundation
