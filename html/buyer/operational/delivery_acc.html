Step 6Invoice Download & GST Report
invoice_compliance
Purpose
Enable buyer to download a digitally-signed GST invoice after balance payment completion. Enable Admin/Finance to generate monthly GST compliance reports, TDS/TCS liability calculations, and export data for statutory filing (GSTR-1, GSTR-2, ITC tracking).
Business Requirement
• BR-135: GST Act Section 31 compliance — digital invoice with GSTIN, HSN code, OTP signature.
• BR-136: Auto-trigger on PO status = "Balance Received".
• BR-137: Reverse charge flagging if buyer GSTIN turnover >₹10Cr.
• BR-139: TCS 206C(1H) @ 0.1% if sales to one buyer >₹50L/year.
• BR-140: Monthly compliance export ready for CA review and GSTR-1 filing.
UX & User Actions
Buyer: PO Detail → "Download Invoice" button appears after balance payment.
Preview invoice (GSTIN, amount, tax breakdown, RC flag) → Download PDF / Print / Email / Share.

Admin/Finance: Select month → Review GST summary → Export GSTR-1 CSV / JSON / PDF for CA filing.
Technical Details
• PDF stored on S3, linked to buyer account, re-downloadable anytime.
• Digital signature: OTP-timestamp watermark by FenBridge Finance.
• RC applicability computed server-side against buyer GSTIN annual turnover register.
• TCS/TDS computed via running annual aggregate per buyer_id.
Database Schema
invoices ( id UUID PK, po_id UUID FK -> purchase_orders.id, invoice_number TEXT UNIQUE, -- FBM-2026-XXXX invoice_date DATE, buyer_id UUID FK, taxable_amount NUMERIC(14,2), gst_rate DECIMAL(4,2), igst_amount NUMERIC(14,2), reverse_charge BOOLEAN, tcs_amount NUMERIC(14,2), digital_signature TEXT, pdf_url TEXT, generated_at TIMESTAMP );
