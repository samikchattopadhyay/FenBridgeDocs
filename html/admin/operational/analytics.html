farmer_cohort_analytics
Purpose
Enable Admin/Finance/Leadership to analyze farmer segments by land size, location, experience, and capability score. Track improvement trends, identify high-risk cohorts, and forecast next-season supply capacity for data-driven decisions on training, marketing, and incentive allocation.
Business Requirements
• BR-129: Segment farmers by land size, location, experience, capability score.
• BR-130: Track capability score month-on-month (target: +5 pts/quarter for new farmers).
• BR-131: Identify high-risk cohorts — >20% quality downgrade, >2 payment disputes, late delivery >30% POs.
• BR-132: Forecast next harvest supply from farmer capacity + historical yield.
• BR-133: ROI tracking — CAC vs LTV by cohort (target: 10:1 LTV:CAC).
• BR-134: Export cohort data for CFO/Board dashboards.
Entry Point
Admin Dashboard → "Analytics" tab → "Farmer Cohorts" · Or: Finance Dashboard → "Supply Forecasting" → "Cohort Analysis".
UX & User Actions
• Select preset cohort (New Farmers, Power Users, At-Risk, High Value) or build custom filter.
• View cohort profile card: size, avg land, avg capability score, geographic distribution.
• Drill into performance metrics: delivery rate, quality acceptance, payout disputes, churn rate.
• Risk segment analysis: High / Medium / Low risk with recommended actions.
• Export CSV/JSON for CA or board review.
Database Schema
farmer_cohort_metrics ( cohort_id TEXT, cohort_name TEXT, farmer_count INT, avg_land_acres DECIMAL(6,2), avg_capability_score DECIMAL(5,2), avg_pos_completed DECIMAL(5,2), on_time_delivery_pct DECIMAL(5,2), quality_acceptance_pct DECIMAL(5,2), payout_dispute_pct DECIMAL(5,2), churn_rate_pct DECIMAL(5,2), ltv_cac_ratio DECIMAL(6,2), period DATE, generated_at TIMESTAMP ); GET /api/v1/analytics/farmer-cohorts?cohort=new_farmers&period=2026-05
