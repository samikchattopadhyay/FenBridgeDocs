Workflow Module 5

Register Farmer (Scout-Assisted)

15-step scout-on-behalf farmer registration wizard. Captures identity, address, first pond, legal documents, bank account with penny-drop verification, and mobile OTP confirmation. All records enter Admin approval queue.

Registration Steps
15 Screens
1Intro & Consent
Purpose
Establishes scout is acting on farmer's behalf with verbal consent recorded.
Data Captured
  • Consent checkbox: "Farmer has given verbal consent"
  • Plain-language explanation displayed
Requirement
Consent mandatory before proceeding; audit trail preserved.
2Farmer Identity Details
Data Captured
  • Full name (mandatory)
  • Date of birth (optional)
  • Mobile (farmer's own, mandatory)
  • Alternate phone (optional)
Validation
Mobile uniqueness check; same mobile cannot be registered twice.
3Mobile Verification — OTP Send
Purpose
Sends OTP to farmer's mobile number to verify ownership.
Data Shown
  • Farmer's mobile number echoed for confirmation
  • "Send OTP" CTA
  • 60-second cooldown on resend
4Mobile Verification — OTP Entry
Purpose
Farmer (with scout present) enters 6-digit OTP received on their mobile.
Requirements
  • Scout reads OTP from farmer's phone in their presence (no proxy allowed)
  • Six-box input, auto-verify on 6th digit
  • 3 retries; 30-second resend timer
  • OTP confirms mobile is real and belongs to farmer
5Language Selection
Purpose
Sets farmer's preferred app language for future app usage.
Options
  • English
  • Hindi
  • Bengali
6Confirm Role = Farmer
Purpose
Final role confirmation before workflow fork into farmer-specific screens.
Role Definition
Farmer: grows raw makhana seed; sells to processors (poppers) or directly to Bharat Makhana.
7Farmer Address Details
Data Captured
  • District (dropdown, mandatory)
  • Panchayat/Village (text)
  • Full address (textarea)
  • Landmark (optional)
Business Requirement
District mandatory—drives scout assignment and broadcast targeting. Residential address often differs from pond address.
8First Pond Details
Data Captured
  • Pond name (optional)
  • Area + unit (Acre/Bigha/Hectare, mandatory)
  • Estimated annual capacity (kg)
Requirement
Farmer must have at least one pond at registration; cannot skip.
9First Pond Legal Documents
Data Captured
  • Document type (Land Record / Pond Lease / Other)
  • Up to 3 photos per document
Requirement
Mandatory—at least one legal document required; proof of pond ownership or lease agreement.
10First Pond Address
Data Captured
  • District (mandatory)
  • Village (mandatory)
  • Free-text address (mandatory)
Note
Pond address often differs from farmer's residential address. Toggle: "Same as farmer's address."
11First Pond Geo-Fencing
Purpose
Captures pond GPS centre point for geo-fencing and task routing.
Requirement
Mandatory—draggable pin on map or "Use my location" shortcut. Offline-capable map required.
Impact
GPS enables automatic task assignment to nearest scout and geofence-based lot verification.
12Bank Account Details
Data Captured
  • Account holder name (mandatory)
  • Account number (entered twice for confirmation)
  • IFSC (auto-resolves bank + branch)
Penny-Drop Verification
Triggered at submit—async ₹1 test transfer; name-match check. Result lands on bank record minutes later. Scout doesn't wait.
Requirement
Only one active bank account per farmer (Year 1); old accounts marked inactive; penny-drop is mandatory for payout eligibility.
13Theme Preference
Options
Dark / Light theme preview tiles. Defaults to Dark.
14Review & Submit
What's Shown
  • Sectioned recap (Identity, Address, Pond, Bank, Documents)
  • Each section has "Edit" link for corrections
Requirement
Explicit final consent checkbox required: "I confirm all details are correct" before submit enabled.
15Submission Confirmation
Success State
Echoes farmer's name, mobile, pond code, and sync status. Single CTA to return to Today Dashboard.
Backend Action
Creates farmers record with status='pending_approval'. Record queued to sync_queue for upload. Penny-drop runs server-side.
Admin Gate
Record in pending state until Admin approves. Farmer cannot transact (bid/list) until approved.
