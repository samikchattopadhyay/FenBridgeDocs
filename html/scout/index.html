Scout Operations Module

Scout Workflows & Operations

Complete inventory of all scout-assisted workflows, on-behalf actions, and operational features. Field scouts manage farmer/popper registrations, perform lot verifications, apply seals, and oversee warehouse quality checks. All workflows are offline-first with pending admin approval gates.

1. Entry & Authentication

Entry & Authentication
3 Screens

Secure mobile + OTP login flow with role-based routing. Scouts authenticate via registered number and OTP, then route to Scout Dashboard.

Screens: Splash · Login · Routing Type: Authentication

2. User Registration & Management

Register Farmer
12 Screens

Scout-assisted farmer profile creation. Captures identity, address, first pond details, legal docs, bank account with penny-drop verification, and mobile OTP confirmation.

Steps: Consent · Mobile OTP · Identity · Language · Address · Pond · Docs · Pond Addr · GPS · Bank · Review · Confirm Gate: Admin Approval Required
Register Popper
15 Screens

Scout-assisted popper (processor) business registration. Captures contact person, business details (PAN/GSTIN/FSSAI), first warehouse, legal documents, bank account with penny-drop verification.

Steps: Consent · Language · Address · Identity · Business · Docs · Business Addr · Warehouse · Warehouse Addr · GPS · Mobile OTP · Bank · Theme · Review · Confirm Gate: Admin Approval Required
Manage Farmer
8 Actions

Edit and manage registered farmer profiles. Supports identity updates, pond management (add/edit/deactivate), bank account changes with penny-drop verification, document uploads, and preference management.

Actions: Edit Identity · Pond Mgmt · Bank · Documents · Preferences Approval: Mobile changes require OTP re-verify
Manage Popper
9 Actions

Manage popper business profiles. Supports business detail updates, warehouse management (add/edit/deactivate), bank and legal document updates, and preference management.

Actions: Business · Warehouse Mgmt · Bank · Documents · Preferences Gate: Business changes flagged to Admin

3. Pond Operations & Intelligence

Pond Inspection
5 Screens

Raw makhana learning intelligence. Scout captures pond conditions, water level, algae presence, pest/disease flags, and 3–5 bund-edge density readings with photos and notes.

Data: GPS · Conditions · Bund Readings · Photos Purpose: Learning-only (Year 1)
Ponds Directory
3 Screens

Browse all assigned ponds with inspection history. Lists ponds scout is assigned to with search, filter by recency, and detailed pond profiles showing history and spot offers.

Features: Search · Filter · History Scope: Scout-assigned ponds only

4. Lot Verification & Quality Control

Lot Verification & Inspection
8 Steps

Multi-step warehouse quality verification. Captures quantity, parameter readings (moisture/foreign matter/defects), defect photos, disposition (Accept/Downgrade/Hold/Reject), and issues grade certificate.

Steps: Quantity · Parameters · Defects · Photos · Disposition · Certificate Outcome: Immutable grade certificate
Seal Application
5 Steps

Tamper-evident lot sealing and spot market listing. Captures unique seal ID, GPS lock, storage check (bags off-floor, lined, segregated), shelf-life (30/45 days), and listing price with premium %.

Data: Seal ID · GPS · Storage Check · Shelf-Life · Price Effect: Lot listed in spot market
Storage Re-check
5 Steps

Mid-window lot condition verification. Light check at ~50% shelf-life for unsealed lots. Verifies seal/net integrity, dry conditions, pest-free state, and determines outcome (Clean/Anomaly/Void).

Trigger: Half shelf-life unsold or anomaly report Outcome: Continue · Re-inspect · Void lot
Re-inspection (Post-Reconditioning)
Multi-step

Second inspection after rejection and reconditioning. Captures prior rejection context, then repeats full verification steps (parameters/defects/photos/disposition). May issue new grade certificate.

Trigger: After lot rejection and supplier reconditioning Output: New verification record · Certificate
Rejection Handling
1 Screen

Post-rejection disposition management. Shows coded reason, supporting photos, and consequence path (Reconditionable → Re-inspect vs Terminal → Closed). Warehouse fitness flagged if needed.

Paths: Reconditionable · Terminal Admin Review: Required for Terminal closures

5. Warehouse Operations & Fitness

Warehouse Fitness Check
5 Screens

One-time and periodic warehouse suitability verification. Assesses 6 criteria (3 critical): dry/cool/shaded, off-floor stacking, no pest/rodent signs, chemical separation, roof/walls sound, adequate ventilation.

Criteria: 6 total (3 critical) Outcome: Pass → Hold lots · Fail → Admin review
Warehouses Directory
2 Screens

Browse all assigned warehouses with fitness status tracking. Lists warehouses scout is assigned to, with search/filter, detailed profiles showing fitness history, active lots, and storage failure roll-up.

Features: Search · Filter · Fitness History Scope: Scout-assigned warehouses only

6. Marketplace Operations (Scout On-Behalf)

Submit Spot Offer
6 Steps

Create spot market listings on behalf of farmers/poppers. Selects source (pond/warehouse), captures lot details, uploads optional photos, and self-assigns scout inspection visit to trigger verification workflow.

Status: Pending verification until scout inspects Sources: Farmer pond · Popper warehouse
Submit Bid On Behalf
6 Steps

Submit bids on buyer requirements on behalf of farmers/poppers. Filters by capability match, selects bid invitation, sets price and ready-by date, picks source pond/warehouse, and submits with Admin approval gate.

Eligibility: Capability match + grade tags Validation: Price ceiling enforcement
Mark Lot Ready
5 Steps

Signal production completion for PO (purchase order) lots. Captures bagged quantity, bag count, mandatory photos (wide + close-up), notifies Admin, and schedules scout verification task.

Triggered By: Farmer indicates ready-to-dispatch Effect: Notifies Admin + schedules verification

7. Dashboard & Core Navigation

Today Dashboard
4 Screens

Scout's home base for daily task management. Shows today's tasks (colour-tagged by type), sync queue status, registered farmers count, and quick FAB for farmer registration. Offline-capable with pull-to-refresh sync.

Features: Tasks · Sync Status · Activity Feed Offline: Full offline-first capability
Registered Users Directory
3 Screens

Browse and manage all farmers and poppers scout registered or is assigned to. Supports search/filter by role/status, with detailed profiles showing ponds/warehouses, bank accounts, documents, and activity history.

Filters: Role · Status (Pending/Active/Suspended) Inline Actions: Edit · Add Pond · Bank · Documents
Sync Queue
1 Screen

Manage offline data uploads. Lists locally captured items awaiting sync (Inspection/Verification/Seal/Registration), shows capture time, photo count, and sync state (Waiting/Syncing/Synced/Conflict). Supports conflict resolution.

Offline Pattern: All workflows capture locally first Conflict Handling: Resolution screen for conflicts
Profile & Settings
6 Options

Scout account management. Supports profile view/edit, assigned areas (read-only), language selection (EN/HI/BN), offline mode toggle, notifications, sync info, and logout with optional password change.

Editable: Name · Language · Theme Sensitive: Mobile requires OTP re-verify
